About me

Published: Mon 13 May 2024
By Demetrio

In misc.

AI Safety and Exploration

I'm deeply intrigued by AI Safety, Self-Discovery, and Exploration. With a background in data science and project management, I've led digital transformation projects across LATAM. Currently, I'm delving into AI safety to lay the groundwork for a research project. My main inspirations in this field are Dan Hendrycks and Ilya Sutskever.

Broadly, my story is as follows:

[2022 - present] I work as a Data Scientist and Project Manager at Exploration and Discovery Technologies leading the digital transformation for consumer packaged goods companies in LATAM.

[2022 - present] I worked as a Data Scientist at Intelimetrica creating geospatial data pipeline for digitial transformation.

[2019 - 2020] I worked as a research assistant, testing models for electronic repulsion integrals optimization in the theoretical chemistry department at the National Autonomous University of Mexico.

[2014 - 2019] I studied Chemistry with orientation in Quantum Chemistry at School of Chemistry at National Autonomous University of Mexico.
